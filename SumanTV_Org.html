How to get the URL from Google Sheets
1
Open your team Google Sheet
2
Go to File → Share → Publish to web
3
Select your sheet tab, change format to Comma-separated values (.csv)
4
Click Publish, copy the URL and paste it above
5
The URL will look like: https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/…/pub?output=csv
6
After connecting, use the ⟳ Sync Sheets button in the header to refresh anytime
CSV Columns: id, name, title, department, reports_to (parent id), photo (URL), location, contact, email, linkedin, instagram, twitter
